Sessions

Youth mentoring session options

Online youth mentoring sessions are designed to feel manageable, supportive, and clear from the beginning. This page gives parents, carers, and young men a simple view of how sessions work and where to start.

Request a free 15-minute consult Back to home

1

Start with an enquiry

Share what is going on, who the support is for, and what you hope mentoring might help with.

2

Free 15-minute first consult

A quick chat to see if this feels like the right fit for the young person or family.

3

Choose the right session

Pick a 30-minute or 60-minute online session and begin at a pace that feels manageable.

4

Build momentum over time

Weekly appointments help build confidence and proactive habits while keeping a clear focus on the goals the client is working towards, helping to build a stronger sense of direction and drive.
