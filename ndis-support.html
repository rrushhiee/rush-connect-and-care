NDIS information

Important details before ongoing support

Rush Connect & Care can support self-managed and plan-managed NDIS participants where the support is appropriate for the participant’s goals, plan and funding type. Support may include community access, routine building, appointment support, social connection, confidence building and practical goal follow-through.

NDIS rules and planning processes are changing, including the introduction of new support needs assessments from mid-2026 and clearer rules about what NDIS funding can be used for. Participants, families, support coordinators and plan managers are encouraged to check the participant’s current plan, funding periods and support categories before booking ongoing support.

Rush Connect & Care works in line with the NDIS Code of Conduct. This service is not clinical therapy, crisis support, emergency response, behaviour support or medical care.
