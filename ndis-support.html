NDIS information

Important details before ongoing support

Rush Connect & Care can support self-managed and plan-managed NDIS participants where the support is appropriate for the participant’s goals, plan, and funding type. The service is intentionally one-to-one, practical, and relationship-based, and may include community access, routine building, appointment support, social connection, confidence building, and practical goal follow-through.

NDIS rules and planning processes are changing, including the introduction of new support needs assessments from mid-2026 and clearer rules about what NDIS funding can be used for. Participants, families, support coordinators, and plan managers are encouraged to check the participant’s current plan, funding periods, and support categories before booking ongoing support.

Rush Connect & Care works in line with the NDIS Code of Conduct. This service is not clinical therapy, crisis support, emergency response, behaviour support, or medical care.

For plan managers and support coordinators

A transparent, specialist referral option

Specialist and relationship-based

Support is built around fit, goals, participant choice, and the value of a steady one-to-one relationship. Referrals are accepted only where the service is appropriate, with no pressure around bundled or unsuitable supports.

Lower-cost and compliant

Current rates are set below current NDIA price limits. For plan-managed participants, invoicing and service delivery are structured to align with the current NDIS Pricing Arrangements and Price Limits, with the relevant support item and claiming rules confirmed before ongoing support begins.

Qualified, screened, and documented

The service combines lived experience with a Diploma of Business, NDIS Worker Screening, and clear referral documents so due diligence is straightforward for plan managers, support coordinators, and families.

The service is guided by the NDIS Code of Conduct and delivered as a small, transparent sole trader service with privacy, honesty, fair pricing, and safe practice at the centre.
