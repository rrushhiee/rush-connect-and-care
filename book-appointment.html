Request a consult or session

Choose a free consult or a mentoring session

Use this page to request a free 15-minute first consult or an online mentoring appointment, choose a preferred date, and note whether you would like a secure payment link, invoice, or another payment arrangement. Only the essentials are needed to request a booking.

How booking works

1

Choose a date

Select a preferred date and a time window that feels realistic for you or your family.

2

Choose a session

Start with a free 15-minute consult or request a 30-minute, 60-minute, or 3-session bundle.

3

Choose payment

Ask for a secure payment link, an invoice and bank transfer, or another agreed payment method.

Appointment request form

All fields are required except the last message box. Your details are only used to arrange the appointment. If you would rather speak first, call 0423 815 267 or email hello@rushconnectandcare.com.au.

Back to session options

Payment options

Secure payment link

Choose this if you would like a simple online payment option after the appointment is confirmed.

A payment portal or secure payment link can be sent directly to you.

Invoice and bank transfer

Choose this if you would prefer to pay from an invoice using bank transfer.

This is often the simplest option for families who want a clear paper trail.

Other payment methods

If you need another arrangement, note it in the form and it can be discussed before the appointment is locked in.
