About

A grounded and respectful approach

As someone who has first-hand experience battling, navigating, and working toward healing from mental health challenges and mental illness, I understand how difficult and complex that journey can be.

Through my own experience, including time spent in hospital, community and rehab environments, I’ve worked hard on my mental health — learning how to build routines, find the right support systems, and navigate important steps from accessing effective services to finding suitable medication and everything in between.

Rush Connect & Care is built around that lived understanding. The focus is on providing genuine, consistent, and approachable support that feels safe and relatable — not clinical or disconnected.

The approach is simple: listen well, build trust, and support each person in taking the next manageable step forward at their own pace.
