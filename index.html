Online support for young people

Youth mentoring and NDIS support with someone steady in your corner

For young people who are struggling, and parents or carers doing their best to help, I offer grounded support built on lived experience, trust, and practical next steps. Through my NDIS support service, I work with men at all stages of life to build confidence, connection, and day-to-day stability.

MENTORING MAY BE A GOOD FIT IF

  • A young person who is feeling overwhelmed, anxious, low, depressed, or stuck
  • Young and struggling to fit in socially or being bullied
  • Getting into trouble at school, work, or home
  • Family dynamic breakdowns, including tension at home, arguments, or breakdowns in communication
  • If you're a young man who lacks goals or direction toward goals

NDIS MAY BE A GOOD FIT IF

  • Men who would benefit from a steady male support worker who brings lived experience, relatability, trust, and practical next steps
  • Men with mental illness who need support with routine, motivation, cleaning, community access, and day-to-day stability
  • Men who are isolated, lacking structure, or struggling to move toward work, study, volunteering, or healthier routines
  • Men who lack transport for groceries, visits, appointments, and everyday practical tasks
  • Men who just need a support worker for a period

NDIS support

What NDIS support can look like

Daily support

Practical help with routine, appointments, everyday tasks, and steady support that helps life feel more manageable.

Community access

Support getting out, building confidence in the community, and staying more connected to the things that matter.

Goal-based support

Support shaped around participant goals, capacity building, and practical progress over time.

Educated and experienced

I am educated and have prior experience working in NDIS, bringing a grounded and informed approach to support.

Support is available for self-managed and plan-managed participants, with a calm, person-centred approach that stays practical and relational.
