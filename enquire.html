Enquire about youth mentoring or NDIS peer support

This form is for online youth mentoring, parent or carer enquiries, and NDIS support for men. If you are a parent looking for support for a young person who feels lost, is acting out, or is struggling with the pressure of becoming an adult, you are welcome to reach out here. A few short details are enough to start.

Enquiry form

Your enquiry is sent to hello@rushconnectandcare.com.au. Required fields are marked by the form. If you would rather speak first, call 0423 815 267.

What happens next

  • Your message is sent directly to Rupert at Rush Connect & Care.
  • You will receive a reply using the email address you provide.
  • If it sounds like the right fit, the next step is a simple first conversation.
