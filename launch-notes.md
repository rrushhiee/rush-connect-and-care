# Rush Connect & Care Launch Notes

## Best low-cost path right now

You do not need a domain or business email to launch a clean first version.

### Option 1: Launch free first

- Publish this site on GitHub Pages for free.
- Use a temporary page URL such as `https://yourusername.github.io/rush-connect-care/`.
- Put your mobile number and current email on the site for first enquiries.
- Buy a custom domain later and point it to the same site.

### Option 2: Buy only the domain first

- When funds are available, buy a domain and keep the same site.
- Cloudflare says its registrar sells domains "at cost" with no markup:
  [Cloudflare Registrar](https://developers.cloudflare.com/registrar/)
- Good domain ideas:
  - `rushconnectcare.com.au`
  - `rushconnectandcare.com.au`
  - `rushconnectcare.com`

### Business email later

- A business email usually needs your own domain first.
- Until then, use a personal email that sounds professional.
- Example: `rush.connect.care@gmail.com` if available.

## Before you go live

- Add your real phone number and email in `index.html`.
- Add your suburb, service area, and whether you offer online support.
- Add your fees or "contact for fees".
- Add your exact qualifications, clear scope, and any referral information.
- Add a short crisis notice if you are not an emergency service.

## Files in this starter site

- `index.html`
- `styles.css`

## Quick preview

Open `index.html` in a browser to review the design locally.
